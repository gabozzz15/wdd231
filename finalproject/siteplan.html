News & Weather Hub - Site Plan

Your one-stop destination for real-time news and weather updates

Site Name

News & Weather Hub

Reason for Selection: The name clearly communicates the dual purpose of the website - providing both news and weather information. "Hub" suggests a central, comprehensive source of information, making it easy for users to understand what the site offers at first glance.

Site Purpose

The News & Weather Hub serves as a comprehensive information portal that integrates real-time news and weather data into a single, user-friendly interface. The website will:

  • Provide up-to-date news articles from various categories (world, technology, sports, business, health)
  • Display current weather conditions and forecasts for user-specified locations
  • Offer search functionality for finding specific news articles
  • Present information in a responsive, accessible design optimized for all devices
  • Allow users to personalize their news feed based on interests
  • Integrate weather alerts with relevant news stories (e.g., storm warnings with emergency news)

Target Audience Scenarios

Business Professional

Scenario: "I need to stay informed about market news and check weather conditions for my upcoming business trips. Can I get both in one place?"

Website Response: The site will provide a business news section and allow users to save multiple locations for weather tracking, helping professionals plan trips and stay market-aware.

Traveler

Scenario: "I'm planning a vacation next week and need to check both travel restrictions in the news and weather forecasts for my destination."

Website Response: Users can search for news about their destination country while simultaneously viewing detailed 7-day weather forecasts for that location.

Student/Researcher

Scenario: "I'm working on a climate change project and need to correlate weather patterns with environmental news articles."

Website Response: The site will allow filtering news by environmental topics while providing historical weather data and climate-related information.

Local Resident

Scenario: "I want to know what's happening in my community today and whether I need an umbrella for my afternoon walk."

Website Response: Local news filtering combined with hyperlocal weather forecasts will provide residents with relevant daily information.

Color Scheme

The color scheme is designed to convey trust, clarity, and professionalism while maintaining visual interest and accessibility.

Primary Blue #1e3a8a Headings, Navigation
Secondary Blue #3b82f6 Buttons, Links
Accent Orange #f97316 Alerts, CTAs
Neutral Light #f8fafc Background
Neutral Dark #1e293b Text, Footer

Color Application

  • Primary Blue (#1e3a8a): Main headings, navigation bar, footer background
  • Secondary Blue (#3b82f6): Buttons, active links, weather widgets, section backgrounds
  • Accent Orange (#f97316): Weather alerts, important notices, hover states, call-to-action elements
  • Neutral Light (#f8fafc): Main background, card backgrounds, content areas
  • Neutral Dark (#1e293b): Body text, footer text, secondary information

Typography

The typography selection prioritizes readability, modern aesthetics, and clear hierarchy.

Montserrat - For Headings

Bold and authoritative for clear content hierarchy

Usage: All headings (H1, H2, H3, H4), navigation items, prominent labels

Inter - For Body Text

Clean, highly readable sans-serif font optimized for screen reading. Excellent for lengthy news articles and detailed weather information.

Usage: Paragraphs, news article content, weather descriptions, form labels, footer text

Font Pairing Rationale

Montserrat provides strong visual impact for headings, while Inter offers exceptional readability for body text. This combination creates a modern, professional appearance suitable for an information-focused website.

Layout Notes

  • Desktop: Two-column layout with main content area and sidebar for additional features
  • Responsive Breakpoints: 640px (mobile to tablet), 1024px (tablet to desktop)
  • Content Priority: Breaking news and current weather given highest visual priority
